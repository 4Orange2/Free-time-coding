Creme Anglaise

  • Time: 1h
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 1L
Eggs 2
Egg yolks 2
Sugar 4 tbsp
Water 250ml

Step 1: Mix eggs, egg yolks and milk on stove at med. Stir until mixed well.

Step 2: Pour water in pan on hot stove set to hi. Gradually add the sugar and mix.

Step 3: Pour caramel sauce on flan.

Cupcakes

  • Time 40 min
  • Serves 12
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Baking powder 2 tsp
Butter 1/2 cup
Sugar 3/4cup
Eggs 2
Milk 1 cup

Step 1:Preheat oven to 375f or 190c; line muffin cups with papers.

Step 2:Cream butter and sugar till light and fluffy (make sure the butter is room temp so the mixture doesn't clump). Beat in eggs one at a time.

Step 3:Add flour (mixed with baking powder and salt) alternating with milk beat well; stir in vanilla.

Divide evenly among pans and bake for 18 minutes. Let cool in pans and enjoy! :).
